Hirepurpose | Designing with Empathy

Summary

About the company

Hirepurpose is a certified B Corporation that helps military veterans and their spouses tranisition into civilian life by helping them find a career they love.

About the
product

Hirepurpose is a platform that allows transitioning service members, veterans and military spouses search and find jobs that match their skillset easily
For employers, the platforms optimizes the candidate search process by providing tools to make the job recruitment process simple.

My role

I was the sole designer in the company and came on-board when they were shifting to a self-service platform on the client side.

Other team members

Since I was the only designer, I worked along the engineering team which consisted of four people (The CTO, VP of Engineering, Front-End Developer, Back-end Developer).

Duration

7 months (Mar 2018- Present)

Process

Understanding the Veteran Space and designign for a niche group of users.

The veteran/military community was absolutely foreign to me before I joined Hirepurpose. I conducted some user interviews to gain a better understanding of our users motivations, goals and frustrations. I also asked a little about their background.
