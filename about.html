About me

Hey I'm Rohini and I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in web development and instructional design. I strive to innovate through a careful balance of intuition and information. I am passionate about impact and believe that great products are formed through a deep understanding of human behavior.

I studied Art History at NYU, where I fell in love with design. Looking and studying amazing works of art made me want to create and build things myself. Building engaging experiences is what drives me and pushes me to become a better designer.

When I'm not designing you can find me getting lost in the Impressionist wing at the MoMA or sipping on some cold brew downtown.
